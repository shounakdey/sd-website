Projects & Research

I am developing a spectral solver utilizing the Chebyshev Spectral method to solve for two-layer RBM convection using the Navier-Stokes and the Cahn-Hilliard equation. The solver aims to bring about spectral accuracy to the combined Cahn-Hilliard-Navier-Stokes solver and serve as a test bench for further problems, including Rayleigh-Taylor instability.
